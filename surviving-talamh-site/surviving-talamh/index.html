The Story

A world where fear is the cage.

That's what everyone believes in Surviving Talamh. A world where fear is used as control, reality is filtered through artificial lenses, and the truth is far more dangerous than anyone realises.

Surviving Talamh is an epic fantasy sci-fi story inspired by Final Fantasy VII, The Matrix, and The Lord of the Rings. But at its core, it's a deeply personal story about fear, identity, and finding the courage to face the truth.

If that sounds like your kind of world, I'd love for you to join me on the journey.
